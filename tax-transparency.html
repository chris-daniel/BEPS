Tax Transparency

Exchange of information is about achieving global tax co-operation through the implementation of international tax standards and other instruments to put an end to bank secrecy and tackle tax evasion.

How it works

Global Forum on Transparency and Exchange of Information for Tax Purposes

The Global Forum is a key international body working on the implementation of international standards on tax transparency. It ensures that these high standards of transparency and exchange of information for tax purposes are in place around the world through its monitoring and peer review activities.

Global Forum

Automatic Exchange Portal

Online support for the implementation of automatic exchange of information in tax matters

Automatic exchange of information is the systematic and periodic transmission of tax information by countries to the residence country concerning various categories of income, such as dividends, interest, gross proceeds, royalties, salaries, pensions, etc. Find out more about the work of the OECD and the Global Forum on Transparency and Exchange of Information for Tax Purposes in this area, including the Common Reporting Standard.
